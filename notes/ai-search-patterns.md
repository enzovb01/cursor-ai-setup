## Tool selection principles (from Indig)

1. **Automate steps, not workflows** — pick tools that slot into a documented bottleneck
2. **Measurement ≠ production** — Omnia/AirOps Insights track visibility; Claude/AirOps produce content
3. **Vibe coding needs dev backup** — marketers prototype; developers productionize
4. **Verification tooling is underspecified** — Indig emphasizes human judgment over any specific QA tool
5. **Engine-specific monitoring** — no single tool covers all three citation surfaces adequately
