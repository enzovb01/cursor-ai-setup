# Recurring Tools

The tools mattered, but they were rarely presented as the solution. Instead, they were used to support specific parts of a much larger workflow.

---

| Tool | Most Common Use |
|------|-----------------|
| ChatGPT | Brainstorming, drafting and research support |
| Claude | Editing and improving long-form content |
| Gemini | Comparing AI responses and validating information |
| Perplexity | Fast research and source discovery |
| Ahrefs | Keyword research, topical authority and SEO analysis |
| Clearscope | Content optimization and AI visibility |
| AirOps | AI-powered SEO workflows and topical authority scoring |

---

One interesting pattern was that no expert relied on a single tool.

Instead, they combined different tools depending on the stage of the workflow. AI handled repetitive tasks, while human expertise remained responsible for strategy, prioritization and final decisions.

That balance appeared much more often than I expected.
