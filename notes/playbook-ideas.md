# Playbook Ideas

> Derived from Kevin Indig research patterns. Each idea maps to a potential playbook module.

## Idea 1: Three-Lane AI Visibility Production System

Split content operations into three engine-specific lanes (Google AIO, ChatGPT, Perplexity) with shared core content but lane-specific distribution, schema, and refresh priorities. Measure presence, portability, and concentration separately per lane instead of a blended AEO score.

**Trigger:** Any team reporting a single "AI visibility score."

---

## Idea 2: Sales-Call → Content Pipeline

Before AI brief generation, pipe winning sales transcripts through an extraction workflow (pain points, objections, competitor mentions). Merge with keyword data. Prioritize topics with high conversion signal and zero/low search volume — the Ramp model at scale.

**Trigger:** Content calendar driven primarily by search volume.

---

## Idea 3: Second-Impression SERP Metadata Layer

Add a parallel optimization pass for title tags, meta descriptions, and structured data designed for SERP re-read (reverse scrolling), not first-scan CTR. Page-type templates: PDP (reviews/ratings), CDP (ItemList/carousels), blog (author entity + dateModified).

**Trigger:** Intent-based content exists but CTR collapses when AIOs appear.

---

## Idea 4: Freshness Factory

Tag all content with last substantive update date. Auto-flag pages >90 days stale. AI-assisted refresh workflow: extract outdated claims → verify against sources → update schema dates → republish. Target quarterly refresh on commercial/high-intent pages.

**Trigger:** LLM citation audits show competitors with newer dates winning citations.

---

## Idea 5: Off-Site Validation Calendar

For every pillar content piece, define paired off-site assets: Reddit thread, YouTube walkthrough, review site presence, digital PR pitch with proprietary data. Track mention frequency per engine alongside on-page metrics.

**Trigger:** Identical on-page optimization yields different AI citation rates vs competitors.

---

## Idea 6: Bottleneck Workflow Mapper

Document the 8–12 step content workflow. Score each step for AI automation suitability. Automate the highest-scoring bottleneck only (usually draft generation or SERP metadata). Keep strategy, fact-check, and brand judgment human with tiered verification gates.

**Trigger:** Team attempting full AI content replacement or no AI adoption at all.

---

## Idea 7: Interactive Content Moat Builder

For each money-page cluster, spec one vibe-coded interactive asset (calculator, assessment, configurator). AI builds MVP → dev productionizes → embed across hub. Measure engagement and conversion lift, not rankings alone.

**Trigger:** Zero-click environment where text-only pages get synthesized by AIOs without clicks.

---

## Idea 8: KPI Migration Roadmap

Phase measurement shift over 2 quarters: add citation rate and mention frequency → add per-engine presence/portability/concentration → deprecate rank-as-primary for AI surfaces. Run tracked prompts 5–10× and average results.

**Trigger:** Leadership asking "what's our AI ranking?" with rank-tracker tooling.
