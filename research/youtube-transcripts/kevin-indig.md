## Video 1: Moving Beyond Old SEO Models in the Age of AI

**Date:** 2025

**Source:** Advanced Web Ranking

**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qujABKOAThA

### Summary

Kevin explains why traditional SEO metrics are becoming less reliable as AI changes how users search. Instead of focusing only on rankings, he encourages marketers to optimize for visibility, trust, and brand presence across AI-powered search experiences.

### Main Ideas

- Rankings alone no longer reflect business impact.
- AI search rewards trusted brands and authoritative entities.
- Visibility across multiple platforms is becoming a core SEO objective.

### Frameworks

- Visibility Economy
- AI Search Optimization

## Video 2: The Future of Search Isn't Search

**Date:** June 18, 2025

**Source:** Marketing Speak Podcast

**URL:** https://www.marketingspeak.com/the-future-of-search-isnt-search-with-kevin-indig/

### Summary

Kevin presents findings from his AI Overviews usability study and explains how search behavior is changing. He argues that trust now influences user decisions before relevance and that SEO teams must rethink how they measure success.

### Main Ideas

- Trust has become the first filter in AI-powered search.
- AI Overviews are accelerating the shift toward zero-click searches.
- Future SEO success depends on influence and brand visibility rather than traffic alone.

### Frameworks

- Trust-before-Relevance
- Zero-Click Search Model

# Overall Video Patterns

- AI is transforming SEO from a ranking discipline into a visibility discipline.
- Brand authority strongly influences AI-generated answers.
- Traditional SEO metrics should be complemented with visibility and citation metrics.
- Multi-channel content strengthens AI search performance.
- Original research creates long-term competitive advantage.

# Knowledge Extracted

## Mental Models

- Visibility is replacing rankings as the primary success metric.
- Trust is evaluated before relevance in AI-assisted search.
- AI search rewards recognized entities over isolated content.

## Best Practices

- Build authority across multiple digital platforms.
- Produce original research to increase credibility.
- Measure visibility, citations and brand presence alongside traffic.

## Actionable Ideas

- Create dashboards that track AI citations in addition to rankings.
- Repurpose research into multiple content formats and channels.
- Prioritize trust-building content over purely keyword-focused articles.
