## Video 1: Product-Led SEO Still Works For AI Search

**Date:** September 30, 2025

**Source:** The Intelligent Marketer

**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_wbGrykRzQA

### Summary

Eli explains why Product-Led SEO remains effective despite the rise of AI Search. Instead of chasing new labels like AEO, he argues that companies should build products and experiences people naturally search for.

### Main Ideas

- Product-Led SEO remains relevant.
- AI changes the search funnel but not user intent.
- Brand reputation influences AI visibility.

## Video 2: Rethinking SEO in the Age of AI

**Date:** 2024

**Source:** Lenny's Podcast

**URL:** https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/rethinking-seo-in-the-age-of-ai-eli-schwartz

### Summary

Eli discusses how AI shifts search toward earlier stages of the customer journey while emphasizing that SEO still dominates mid-funnel discovery.

### Main Ideas

- AI owns more top-of-funnel interactions.
- Mid-funnel SEO remains essential.
- Growth channels should complement each other.

### Frameworks

- Mid-Funnel SEO

# Knowledge Extracted

## Mental Models

- Product value creates organic growth.
- Search follows customer intent.
- AI extends SEO instead of replacing it.

## Best Practices

- Design products with search in mind.
- Focus on customer journeys.
- Build reputation alongside rankings.

## Actionable Ideas

- Create SEO roadmaps from product flows.
- Measure mid-funnel organic performance.
