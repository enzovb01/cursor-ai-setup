## Post 1: SEO vs GEO

**Date:** April 2025

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/aleydasolis/

### Summary

Aleyda explains the differences between traditional SEO and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). Instead of treating them as competing disciplines, she argues that GEO builds upon strong SEO fundamentals while adapting to AI-powered search experiences.

### Key Takeaways

- GEO complements traditional SEO rather than replacing it.
- Technical SEO remains critical for AI visibility.
- Strong content architecture benefits both search engines and LLMs.
