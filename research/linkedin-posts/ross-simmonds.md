## Post 2: Distribution, Not Content, Was the Problem

**Date:** 2026

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rosssimmonds_every-marketer-has-had-this-moment-you-activity-7445892892096745472-alZk

### Summary

Ross argues that most marketing teams don't have a content problem—they have a distribution problem. Great content often underperforms simply because it isn't systematically repurposed and distributed.

### Key Takeaways

- Distribution deserves as much attention as content creation.
- Repurpose content across multiple channels.
- Every content asset should have a distribution plan.

### Frameworks

- Create Once, Distribute Forever

# Overall LinkedIn Patterns

- Distribution is as important as creation.

- Every content asset should be repurposed.

- Modern SEO depends on content visibility, not only rankings.

# Knowledge Extracted

## Mental Models

- Content creation is only half of the marketing process.
- Distribution compounds the value of every asset.
- Originality becomes more valuable as AI adoption increases.

## Best Practices

- Repurpose every high-performing asset.
- Build distribution into the content workflow.
- Prioritize unique insights over publishing volume.

## Actionable Ideas

- Create a distribution checklist for every article.
- Turn one long-form asset into multiple formats.
- Measure content ROI across all distribution channels.
