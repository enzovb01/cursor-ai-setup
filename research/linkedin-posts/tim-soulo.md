## Post 3: The era of evergreen SEO content is over

**Date:** 2025

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/timsoulo_the-era-of-evergreen-seo-content-is-over-activity-7374785611272024064-GpgG

### Summary

Tim argues that SEO content now requires ongoing updates because AI-driven search systems constantly re-evaluate relevance and freshness. Static evergreen content is less reliable in a fast-changing search environment.

### Key Takeaways

- Freshness is becoming a stronger visibility signal.
- Content refresh workflows are essential.
- AI search rewards updated and relevant information.

### Frameworks

- Real-time SEO
- Content Refresh Strategy

# Overall LinkedIn Patterns

- Tim consistently pushes back against low-quality AI content shortcuts.
- Ahrefs uses original data to validate AI Search assumptions.
- Content quality, freshness and distribution remain central to SEO.
- AI changes execution speed but not the need for strategic judgment.
- Sustainable content growth depends on systems, not hacks.
