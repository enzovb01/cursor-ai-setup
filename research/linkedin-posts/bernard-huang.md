## Post 3: Prompt Tracking is the New Rank Tracking

**Date:** August 2025

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/bernardjhuang_if-seo-tracking-keywords-then-aeo-tracking-activity-7424551520656039936-Lhe1

### Summary

Bernard introduces Prompt Tracking as a way to monitor how AI systems respond to relevant user prompts. Instead of tracking keyword rankings, marketers should observe brand visibility across AI-generated answers.

### Key Takeaways

- Prompt Tracking complements keyword tracking.
- AI visibility should be measured across multiple prompts.
- Citation frequency is becoming a key KPI.

- AI Visibility Measurement
