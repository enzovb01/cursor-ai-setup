# Kevin Indig

## Podcast 1

**Title:** The Future of Search Isn't Search

**Host:** Marketing Speak

**Date:** June 18, 2025

**URL:** https://www.marketingspeak.com/the-future-of-search-isnt-search-with-kevin-indig/

### Summary

Kevin discusses how AI is reshaping search, the importance of trust in AI-generated answers and why marketers should focus on visibility beyond traditional search rankings.

### Key Takeaways

- AI changes how users discover information.
- Brand trust influences AI citations.
- SEO is evolving into AI Search Optimization.
