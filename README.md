# Phase 2 — AI-Powered SEO Content Production Research

## Objective

This repository explores how leading SEO professionals are adapting their content strategies to AI Search. Rather than summarizing individual resources, the objective was to identify recurring patterns, practical frameworks and reusable knowledge that could later be transformed into an internal playbook.

## Research Process

I reviewed content from ten industry experts across LinkedIn, YouTube, newsletters, podcasts and articles.

Rather than documenting every source, I focused on identifying recurring ideas, practical frameworks and reusable knowledge. The `notes` section represents my own synthesis after comparing insights across all of the research.

---

## Key Takeaway

After reviewing all ten experts, the biggest takeaway wasn't discovering a new AI tool or a new SEO tactic. Instead, it was realizing that they consistently returned to the same principles: understand the audience, create original value, use AI intentionally and continuously improve the content process.

Although AI Search is changing how people discover information, the strongest strategies still rely on the same fundamentals.
